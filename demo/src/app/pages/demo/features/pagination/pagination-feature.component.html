Pagination

Break large datasets into manageable pages with customizable page sizes. The pagination control shows the current range, total items, and provides navigation controls.

Real-World Example: Order Management System

A typical e-commerce order management table with 47 orders. Users can navigate through pages and adjust the number of items per page (5, 10, 20, or 50). The pagination shows "X - Y (pageSize) of Total Items" on the left, with page controls and page size selector on the right.
